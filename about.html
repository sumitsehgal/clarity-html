About us

Clarity Medical is India's only pure play medical electronics company. It was founded in 2003 by a group of engineers and entrepreneurs. Our management and engineering team has over a century of combined experience in hardware, software and electro-mechanical design specifically in the medical device industry. We offer a comprehensive range of medical equipment - from patient monitors to various function testing equipments (Like EMG - Stress Test - Spirometer etc., EEG). In addition, Clarity Medical offers various sub-system modules (SpO2, NiBP, ECG, etc) with embedded software to OEMs for further integration into their products.

Our mission is to make high quality healthcare technology available at affordable prices to a vast majority of the population of emerging countries in Asia, Latin America, Africa and Eastern Europe. To achieve this goal, the R&D team at Clarity Medical has developed advanced signal processing algorithms, new sensor designs and other innovative techniques to manufacture cost effective medical devices and software for use in Cardiology, Neurology, Patient Monitoring and other clinical applications.

Our Major product lines are Patient Monitors, Pulse Oximeters, ECG Machines, EEG Machines and EMG/NCS/EP Machines. These products have been developed and manufactured entirely in India by highly qualified, experienced and dedicated R&D personnel and production teams. Clarity Medical is the only medical device company based in India that designs and develops its products using innovative technologies created by its in-house R&D team. Currently, Clarity Medical is successfully selling its products in India to large and small hospitals, prestigious medical research institutes, diagnostic centers, doctors and consultants through a nation-wide dealer network. In the near future, Clarity Medical plans to expand its distribution and service network within India and overseas to cater to other emerging countries in Asia, Latin America, Africa and Eastern Europe.
